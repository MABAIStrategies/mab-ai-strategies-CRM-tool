CRM How-to Guide (Single Page)

This guide is your fast-start operating playbook for the MAB AI Strategies CRM. Use it to capture opportunities, run strategy, and automate client momentum from one interface.

1) Daily workflow

Start in Today

Open /today first to review open tasks, overdue actions, and current deal momentum. Clear urgent blockers before creating new work.

Capture in Workspace

Use /workspace for rapid notes, AI summaries, and action extraction. Attach outputs to Companies, Contacts, and Deals in the same session.

Advance in Finish Line

Use /finish-line to align capture, memory, assets, and compliance actions into one strategic progress view.

2) Recommended setup sequence

  1. Create and verify Companies and Contacts records.
  2. Open Deals with clear next steps and ownership.
  3. Set Tasks with deadlines for follow-up commitments.
  4. Upload strategic sales assets in /assets.
  5. Test semantic recall in /search to confirm memory quality.
  6. Use /brainstorming and /superpowers to prioritize improvements.

3) Automation best practices

AI output discipline

Keep each note objective-focused: challenge, impact, decision, and next action. Better structure yields better drafts, summaries, and reminders.

Compliance-first outreach

Require review checkpoints before outbound automation. Use the CRM compliance panels to prevent accidental sends.

Pipeline hygiene

Never let a deal sit without a dated next step. Update stage, owner, and confidence notes after every meaningful interaction.
