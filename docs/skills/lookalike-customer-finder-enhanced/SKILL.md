# Lookalike Customer Finder (Enhanced)

Find ideal prospects + track what actually converts to create a self-improving prospecting system.

## ENHANCEMENT: Persistent ICP Memory

### Store ICP Versions Over Time
```
ICP Evolution:
- ICP_v1 (Jan): Mid-market SaaS, $5-10M, Series A-B
- ICP_v2 (Feb): Added "Hiring 20+ in last 90 days" (highest signal)
- ICP_v3 (Mar): Weighted funding signals higher (40% of wins)
```

### Track Tier Performance
- **Tier 1 (90-100 similarity)**: 28% closed → 35% after ICP v2 update
- - **Top Converting Signal**: "Recent funding round"
  - - **Least Converting**: "Uses Salesforce" (too generic)
   
    - ### Monthly Improvement Cycle
    - 1. Contact prospects from this month's ICP
      2. 2. Track which scores/signals convert
         3. 3. Update ICP with highest-converting characteristics
            4. 4. Re-score next month's prospects with improved weighting
               5. 5. Watch conversion rate improve
                 
                  6. ### Connected to Your CRM
                  7. Your persistent ICP becomes source of truth for:
                  8. - **Outbound targeting**: Who to prospect
                     - - **Lead scoring**: Rate inbound leads against ICP
                       - - **Customer segmentation**: Which tier did they come from?
                         - - **Churn risk**: Customers matching old ICP may be at risk
                          
                           - ## Core Workflow
                          
                           - ### 1. Build Your ICP (5 Dimensions)
                           - - Firmographics: Industry, size, revenue, stage, geography
                             - - Tech Stack: Common technologies + adoption %
                               - - Growth Signals: Funding, hiring, expansion, launches
                                 - - Buying Behavior: Decision makers, deal size, sales cycle
                                   - - Psychographics: Culture, values, priorities
                                    
                                     - ### 2. Find 100+ Lookalikes
                                     - Weighted scoring model:
                                     - - Industry Match: 25%
                                       - - Company Size: 20%
                                         - - Tech Stack: 15%
                                           - - Growth Stage: 15%
                                             - - Revenue Range: 15%
                                               - - Geography: 10%
                                                
                                                 - Similarity Scale: 90-100 (perfect), 80-89 (strong), 70-79 (good)
                                                
                                                 - ### 3. Tier & Target
                                                 - - **Tier 1** (90-100): Top 10 prospects, highly personalized (40-50% response)
                                                   - - **Tier 2** (80-89): Next 40, personalized at scale (20-30% response)
                                                     - - **Tier 3** (70-79): Next 50, volume outreach (10-15% response)
                                                      
                                                       - ### 4. Track & Improve
                                                       - Record for each prospect contacted:
                                                       - - Similarity score
                                                         - - Response/meeting rate
                                                           - - Deal stage progression
                                                             - - Win/loss outcome
                                                              
                                                               - Analyze monthly: Which scores/signals correlate with closes?
                                                              
                                                               - ### 5. Update ICP Quarterly
                                                               - Based on win/loss analysis:
                                                               - - Up-weight signals present in most wins
                                                                 - - Down-weight signals present in most losses
                                                                   - - Create ICP v2 with new weightings
                                                                     - - Re-score all prospects in pipeline
                                                                       - - Watch metrics improve
                                                                        
                                                                         - ## Best Practices
                                                                        
                                                                         - ✅ Quality over quantity (10 perfect > 100 mediocre)
                                                                         - ✅ Track performance + close the loop
                                                                         - ✅ Update ICP monthly based on wins
                                                                         - ✅ Use multiple matching dimensions
                                                                         - ✅ Prioritize recent growth signals
                                                                        
                                                                         - ❌ Don't match only on industry
                                                                         - ❌ Don't ignore performance data
                                                                         - ❌ Don't use static ICPs (update seasonally)
                                                                        
                                                                         - ## Use Cases
                                                                        
                                                                         - **"Find 100 companies like my top 5 customers"**
                                                                         - > Input: Top customers + their key characteristics
                                                                           > > Output: Ranked lookalike list + ICP + contact intelligence + targeting strategy
                                                                           > > > Bonus: Track conversion by tier for next month's improvements
